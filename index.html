    ENTERING MEDIUM
    
  
    
    MEDIUM is an experimental tool: it stirs a fluid field with your hands and lets the currents themselves play a second granular voice alongside your own playing.
  
    
  
    
    Move your hands through the frame to stir the field. Density and direction of your gestures shape where the current flows and what it triggers — there's no fixed mapping to learn, just push and watch it respond.
  
    
  
    It can behave unpredictably — that's the point. Turn it off any time from the same button.
