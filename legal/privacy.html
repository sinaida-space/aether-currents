Privacy

AETHER CURRENTS does not use cookies, does not run analytics, and has no accounts. There is no server behind this tool beyond the static files you're reading right now.

Camera

Your camera feed is processed entirely inside your browser, on your device, using the on-device hand-tracking model. Video frames are never transmitted anywhere, never stored, and never leave your machine.

localStorage

Two keys are written to your browser's local storage, and nothing else:

  • ac.consent — whether you've acknowledged this notice
  • ac.mode — your last chosen mode (FULL or LIGHT)

Clearing your browser storage removes both. No other data is written.

Third-party model files

The hand-tracking pipeline loads its model files from Google's MediaPipe CDN. That request is a standard file fetch — Google's servers see a normal HTTP request for a static asset, nothing about your session, your camera, or your usage. No video or gesture data is ever sent there.
